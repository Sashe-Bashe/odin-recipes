World's Best Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients: A top-down view of all ingredients needed for lasagna gathered on a kitchen countertop.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce:
    • Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water.
    • Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper.
    • Simmer, covered, for about 1 ½ hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Prepare the lasagna noodles:
    • Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
    • Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes.
    • Drain noodles and rinse with cold water.
  4. Prepare the cheese mixture: In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  5. Preheat the oven: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  6. Assemble the lasagna:
    • Spread 1 ½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    • Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, slightly overlapping.
    • Spread 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture over noodles.
    • Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices.
    • Spoon another 1 ½ cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
    • Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
    • Cover with foil. To prevent sticking, spray the foil with cooking spray or ensure it doesn't touch the cheese.
  7. Bake the lasagna:
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes with foil on.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until golden-brown.
  8. Rest and serve: Allow the lasagna to rest for 15 minutes before serving.
